# Shift Summary

Shift summaries provide an overview of member activity within an organization, grouped by shift type.

## What are Shift Types?

See [Shift Types documentation](shift_types.md) for more information on shift types. Shift summaries rely on shift types to categorize and summarize member participation effectively.

## Purpose of Shift Summaries

The shift summary aggregates the participation and activity of members, broken down by shift type. This allows organizations to:

- Track how many shifts of each type a member has completed.
- Identify areas where more volunteers or staff may be needed.
- Recognize members who contribute significantly to specific types of shifts.

Shift summaries are a valuable tool for both organizational planning and member recognition.
